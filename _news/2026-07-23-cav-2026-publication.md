Together with [Tobias Kappé](https://tobias.kap.pe), I co-authored
"[Octopus: Practical Equivalence Checking of P4 Packet Parsers](https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-032-32519-8_11)", which
was published at the
[International Conference on Computer Aided Verification (CAV 2026)](https://conferences.i-cav.org/2026/) in Lisbon,
Portugal. The paper extends the Octopus tool from my bachelor's thesis and represents my first peer-reviewed
publication.
