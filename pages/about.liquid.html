Welcome to my personal website! I'm Jort van Leenen, a MSc Computer Science student at Leiden University, The Netherlands.

From 2021 to 2025, I studied Computer Science (Dutch: Informatica) at Leiden University. During the first two years, I followed the regular curriculum. In my third year, I mainly took courses from the BSc Data Science & Artificial Intelligence programme. In my fourth and final year, I completed my BSc in Computer Science. Taking the additional third year was a valuable experience, giving me a broad understanding of said field. I obtained my Bachelor's degree in 2025 at Leiden University.

I am passionate about theoretical computer science and its real-world applications. I am currently pursuing the Foundations of Computing track of the MSc.

Feel free to explore my website to learn more about my projects, skills, and experiences. If you have any questions or would like to get in touch, please don't hesitate to contact me using the information provided in the sidebar.
